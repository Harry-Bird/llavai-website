# Pricing — Llavai

Llavai is an AI rental concierge for Barcelona. Prices are in EUR. Plan prices are per month.
Sign up: https://www.llavai.com/get-started

## Essential — €19 / month
Self-serve. You make the calls, but you see the best flats first.
- Every new Barcelona listing, monitored for you
- Each listing scored for appeal, so you skip the duds
- The facts that matter, laid out clearly
- One-tap calling: the agency's number, right in the feed

## Pro — €185 / month (by application)
Done for you. Julia, the AI voice agent, calls agents in native Barcelona Spanish so you never have to.
- Everything in Essential, plus:
- Julia calls every quality listing for you: around 60 a month (~15 a week)
- Within ~60 seconds of a new listing, in native Barcelona Spanish
- She presents your tenant profile and asks for a viewing on every call
- Top up any time with call packs

## Call packs — pay as you go
Each credit is one quality listing Julia calls for you, in native Spanish. Add them on top of
Essential, or top up Pro from your account, any time.
- 1 call — €12
- 5 calls — €49
- 15 calls — €119 (best value)
